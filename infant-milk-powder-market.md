<p><p>The infant milk powder market offers various types of formulas catering to different needs. Cow's milk-based formula is the most common and suitable for the majority of infants. Soy-based formula is designed for babies with lactose intolerance or cow's milk allergy. Protein hydrolysate-based formula is developed for infants with a higher risk of developing allergies. Specialty formula is intended for babies with specific conditions, such as preterm birth, low birth weight, or metabolic disorders. These different formulas provide options and alternatives for parents based on their baby's dietary requirements and health conditions.</p></p>
