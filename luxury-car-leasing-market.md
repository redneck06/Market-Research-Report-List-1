<p><p>The luxury car leasing market encompasses three main types: short-term rental, long-term rental, and finance leasing. Short-term rental refers to leasing a luxury car for a brief period, usually a few days or weeks. Long-term rental involves leasing a luxury car for an extended duration, typically several months. Finance leasing refers to a leasing arrangement where the customer doesn't intend to purchase the vehicle but agrees to make fixed payments over a predetermined period, often with an option to buy at the end of the term.</p></p>
