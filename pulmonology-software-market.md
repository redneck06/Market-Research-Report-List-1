<p><p>Pulmonology software market includes three main types: measurement software, analysis software, and management software. Analysis software assists in interpreting and analyzing the collected data, providing insights and diagnostics for respiratory conditions. Management software aims to facilitate the storage, organization, and retrieval of patient data, offering features like patient scheduling, electronic health records, and data integration. Overall, these software types are essential for efficient and accurate management of pulmonary health information.</p></p>
