<p><p>Ear specula are medical devices used to examine the ear canal. They come in two types: sterile and non-sterile. Sterile ear specula are individually packaged and are meant to be used once and then discarded. They are commonly used in hospitals, clinics, and other healthcare settings where the risk of infection is high. Non-sterile ear specula, on the other hand, are reusable and can be cleaned and sterilized before each use. They are often used in private practices and healthcare facilities with lower infection risk. Both types serve the same purpose of facilitating safe and accurate ear examinations.</p></p>
