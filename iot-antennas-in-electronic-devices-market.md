<p><p>IoT antennas in electronic devices market comprise various types, including chip antennas, wire antennas, whip antennas, PCB antennas, and proprietary antennas. Chip antennas are compact and integrate directly onto the PCB, making them suitable for small devices. Wire antennas are simple and cost-effective, while whip antennas offer higher performance and a longer range. PCB antennas are printed onto the circuit board, providing space-saving features. Proprietary antennas are customized for specific applications and offer unique functionalities. These antenna types cater to different requirements and enable connectivity in IoT devices.</p></p>
