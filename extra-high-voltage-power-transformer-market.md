<p><p>Extra High Voltage Power Transformers are categorized based on their voltage capacity into three main types: 230-320KV, 320-550KV, and 550-1000KV. The 230-320KV transformers are commonly used in urban and industrial areas, while the 320-550KV transformers are utilized in intercity electricity transmission. The highest voltage capacity transformers, 550-1000KV, are typically used for long-distance power transmission and large-scale industrial applications. Each type serves specific purposes based on the required voltage levels and electricity transmission needs.</p></p>
