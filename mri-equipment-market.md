<p><p>MRI equipment refers to Magnetic Resonance Imaging equipment, which is used for diagnostic imaging purposes in the medical field. It uses a strong magnetic field and radio waves to generate detailed images of the organs and tissues within the body. <p><strong>The MRI Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Superconducting Type MRI Equipment</li><li>Permanent-magnet Type MRI Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MRI Equipment market can be divided into two types: superconducting type and permanent-magnet type. Superconducting type MRI Equipment utilizes superconducting magnets, which generate a strong magnetic field, allowing for high-quality images. These systems typically require cooling with liquid helium to maintain their superconductivity. On the other hand, permanent-magnet type MRI Equipment utilizes permanent magnets to generate the necessary magnetic field. They do not require cooling, making them more cost-effective and easier to maintain. Both types play a crucial role in healthcare settings for diagnostic purposes.</p></p>
