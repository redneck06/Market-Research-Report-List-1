<p><p>Hemodialysis catheters are used for vascular access during hemodialysis procedures. There are two types of hemodialysis catheters available in the market: long-term and short-term catheters. Long-term catheters are designed for extended or permanent use, typically lasting up to several weeks or months. On the other hand, short-term catheters are used for temporary access during acute dialysis treatments, generally lasting for a few days. Both types of catheters play a crucial role in providing reliable and efficient vascular access for hemodialysis patients.</p></p>
