<p><p>The power supply equipment market consists of two main types: power supply systems and transmission and distribution systems. Power supply systems refer to the equipment that generates and supplies electric power to various end-users. This can include power plants, renewable energy systems, and backup power systems. On the other hand, transmission and distribution systems are responsible for the transportation of electric power from the generation source to the end-users. This involves the infrastructure of power lines, substations, transformers, and distribution networks. Both of these systems are crucial in ensuring the reliable and efficient supply of electricity.</p></p>
