<p><p>Nanoimprint Lithography Equipment is used for printing nanoscale patterns on various materials. There are different types of equipment available in the market. Hot Embossing (HE) is a technique where a heated mold is pressed onto the material. UV-based Nanoimprint Lithography (UV-NIL) uses UV light to cure a liquid resin. Micro Contact Printing (µ-CP) involves transferring ink from a patterned stamp onto the material. These equipment types offer different advantages in terms of resolution, speed, and material compatibility for nanoimprint lithography applications.</p></p>
