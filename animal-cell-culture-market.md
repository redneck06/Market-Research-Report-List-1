<p><p>Animal Cell Culture Market Types:</p><p>1. Classical Media & Salts: Classical media and salts are commonly used in animal cell culture to provide essential nutrients and maintain the pH balance of the culture. These media contain a mixture of inorganic salts, amino acids, vitamins, and other nutrients necessary for cell growth and proliferation.</p><p>2. Serum-free Media: Serum-free media are designed to eliminate the need for animal-derived serum, which can contain various growth factors and hormones that may interfere with specific research goals. These media are optimized to support cell growth and survival by providing all the necessary nutrients and growth factors in a defined and controlled manner.</p><p>3. Stem Cell Media: Stem cell media are specially formulated to support the growth and differentiation of stem cells in culture. These media contain specific factors, such as cytokines and growth factors, which help maintain the pluripotency or promote the differentiation of stem cells into desired cell types. Stem cell media are crucial for research and applications in regenerative medicine and tissue engineering.</p></p>
