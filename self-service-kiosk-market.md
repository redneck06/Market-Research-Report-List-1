<p><p>A self-service kiosk is a standalone interactive device that allows users to perform specific tasks or transactions without the need for human intervention. AI-powered kiosks can analyze user data and provide personalized recommendations, enhancing the overall customer experience. <p><p>The self-service kiosk market can be categorized into two main types: indoor kiosks and outdoor kiosks. Indoor kiosks are designed for installation inside buildings such as malls, airports, and offices. They provide convenient access to various services like ticketing, information, and purchases. Outdoor kiosks, on the other hand, are specifically built to withstand outdoor conditions and are typically found in public places such as streets, parks, and transportation hubs.
