<p><p>Genetically Modified Organisms (GMO) testing market can be broadly categorized into two types: Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) technology and Immunoassay technology. PCR technology involves amplifying and detecting specific DNA sequences to identify the presence of GMOs. It offers high sensitivity and accuracy. On the other hand, immunoassay technology detects GMOs by utilizing specific antibodies that bind to GMO proteins. It provides rapid results with minimal sample preparation. Both technologies play a vital role in ensuring the safety and regulatory compliance of GMO products in various industries such as food, agriculture, and healthcare.</p></p>
