<p><strong>The Partial Discharge Monitoring Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Permanent Monitoring System</li><li>Temporary Monitoring System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Partial discharge monitoring systems are used in electrical systems to detect and monitor partial discharges, which can be early indicators of insulation deterioration or equipment failure. The permanent monitoring system is a continuous monitoring solution that is permanently installed in the electrical system to provide real-time data and alerts. On the other hand, temporary monitoring systems are portable devices that are used for temporary monitoring purposes, such as during maintenance or testing activities. Both systems offer valuable insights into the condition of the electrical system and help in preventing unexpected failures.</p></p>
