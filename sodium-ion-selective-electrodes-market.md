<p><strong>The Sodium Ion Selective Electrodes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Liquid Membrane</li><li>PVC Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sodium ion selective electrodes are devices used for measuring the concentration of sodium ions in a solution. There are two main types of electrodes in the market: liquid membrane and PVC membrane. Liquid membrane electrodes consist of a liquid ion-exchange solution embedded in a polymer matrix, allowing the selective passage of sodium ions. PVC membrane electrodes, on the other hand, use a polyvinyl chloride membrane that is doped with specific sodium ion carriers, enabling the selective detection of sodium ions. Both types offer accurate sodium ion measurements for various applications.</p></p>
