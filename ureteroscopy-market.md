Ureteroscopy allows doctors to visualize the internal structures of the urinary tract and perform a range of treatments such as removing kidney stones, treating strictures or tumors, and addressing other urological conditions.</p><p>The ureteroscopy market has been experiencing significant growth in recent years due to several factors. <p><p>The Ureteroscopy market consists of two main types of ureteroscopes: Single-Use Ureteroscope and Reusable Ureteroscope. A Single-Use Ureteroscope is designed for one-time use, providing convenience and eliminating the need for reprocessing. It minimizes the risk of cross-contamination and reduces downtime for cleaning and sterilization. On the other hand, Reusable Ureteroscopes are designed to be reprocessed and used multiple times. They are more cost-effective in the long run but require thorough cleaning, disinfection, and sterilization between uses to ensure patient safety.</p></p>
