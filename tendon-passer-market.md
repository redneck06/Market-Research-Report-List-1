<p><p>Tendon passers are medical devices used in orthopedic surgeries to pass tendons through bone tunnels. They are available in two market types: straight and curved. Straight tendon passers have a linear shape, allowing for easy passage of tendons along a straight path. These different market types provide surgeons with options to choose the most suitable tendon passer based on the specific requirements of the surgical procedure.</p></p>
