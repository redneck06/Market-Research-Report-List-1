<p><p>Energy recovery ventilator (ERV) units are devices used to improve indoor air quality by supplying fresh air while simultaneously removing stale air from a building. ERV units come in three main types: wall-mount, ceiling-mount, and cabinet-mount. Wall-mount ERV units are installed on exterior walls, while ceiling-mount units are mounted on the ceilings. Cabinet-mount ERV units are designed to be installed in cabinets or closets. These different types cater to various installation requirements and allow for efficient air exchange, ensuring a healthier and more comfortable indoor environment.</p></p>
