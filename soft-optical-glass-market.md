<p><p>Soft optical glass is a type of glass that is characterized by its high refractive index and low dispersion properties. Manufacturers are investing in research and development activities to enhance the properties of soft optical glass, such as reducing its weight and thickness without compromising on its optical performance. This trend is driven by the increasing demand for lightweight and portable devices in various industries.</p><p>Another trend in the soft optical glass market is the growing demand for high-resolution imaging systems.
