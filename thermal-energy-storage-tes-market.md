<p><ul><li>Sensible Heat Regenerative</li><li>Latent Heat Storage</li><li>Thermochemical Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermal Energy Storage (TES) is a technology that involves storing thermal energy for use at a later time when needed. There are different types of TES markets. Sensible Heat Regenerative TES involves storing heat in a solid or liquid material that can be regenerated by extracting the heat later. Latent Heat Storage TES involves storing heat by changing a material's phase from solid to liquid or liquid to gas and vice versa. Thermochemical Mode TES involves storing heat by using a chemical reaction to release or absorb heat energy.</p></p>
