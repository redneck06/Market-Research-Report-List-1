<p><p>E-Pharmacy refers to the online platform where individuals can purchase medicines and other healthcare products. The market can be classified into two main types: on-premise and cloud-based. In contrast, cloud-based e-pharmacy operates solely online, where customers can browse through a wide range of products, make purchases, and have them delivered to their doorstep.
