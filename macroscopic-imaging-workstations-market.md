<p><p>Macroscopic imaging workstations are utilized for capturing and analyzing large-scale images of objects or specimens. There are two main types of macroscopic imaging workstations: image acquisition and digital type market. Image acquisition workstations focus on the process of capturing images through various devices such as cameras, microscopes, or scanners. These workstations employ advanced technologies to ensure high-quality image acquisition. On the other hand, digital type market workstations primarily revolve around storing, managing, and analyzing the acquired images using digital tools and software. They enable users to enhance, measure, annotate, and analyze images for research and diagnostic purposes.</p></p>
