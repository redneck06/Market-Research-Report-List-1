<p><p>Gas phase filtration refers to the process of removing harmful gases and foul odors from the air. Activated carbon filter is a commonly used type that employs a highly porous form of carbon to trap and absorb pollutants. Activated alumina filter, on the other hand, uses a granular material made of aluminum oxide to remove impurities. Other types of gas phase filtration may include adsorbent beds, molecular sieves, and pleated filters, among others. These filters play a crucial role in maintaining clean and breathable air in various industrial, commercial, and residential settings.</p></p>
