<p><p>The Anti-thrombin III Testing Market includes three main types: Diagnosis of antithrombin deficiency, Activity assays, and Immunological assays. The diagnosis of antithrombin deficiency involves identifying individuals with low levels of antithrombin III, a protein that inhibits blood clotting. Activity assays measure the functionality of antithrombin III by assessing its ability to inhibit blood clotting. Immunological assays detect the presence of antithrombin III in a sample using specific antibodies. These tests are crucial in diagnosing and monitoring conditions related to abnormal blood clotting.</p></p>
