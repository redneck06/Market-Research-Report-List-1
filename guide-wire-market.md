<p><p>The guide wire market is used in the treatment of Peripheral Artery Disease (PAD) and Cardiovascular Diseases. PAD refers to the narrowing or blockage of arteries in the limbs due to buildup of plaque, causing reduced blood flow. Guide wires are used in procedures such as angioplasty to navigate through the narrowed arteries and place stents or balloons to restore blood flow. Similarly, in cardiovascular diseases, guide wires help in navigating through the blood vessels and perform interventions like stent placement to treat blockages in the heart or other vital organs.</p></p>
