<p><p>Job search engines can be categorized into two market types- cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based job search engines operate on remote servers, allowing users to access and utilize the platform via the internet. This offers convenience and scalability as users can access the platform from anywhere, anytime. On-premises job search engines, on the other hand, are installed and operated locally within an organization's infrastructure. This setup provides companies with greater control and security but limits accessibility to a specific physical location.</p></p>
