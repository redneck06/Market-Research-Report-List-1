<p><ul><li>Cloud-based</li><li>On-premises</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cemetery Management System Market Types refer to two different deployment models for managing cemetery operations. Cloud-based systems are hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet, allowing users to manage cemetery data and operations from anywhere with an internet connection. On-premises systems, on the other hand, are installed and maintained on local servers within the cemetery premises, requiring users to be physically present on-site for accessing and using the system. These types depend on the chosen infrastructure for cemetery management, with cloud-based systems offering more flexibility and accessibility, while on-premises systems provide greater control and customizability.</p></p>
