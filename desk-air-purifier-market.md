<p><p>Passive desk air purifiers rely on filters to remove pollutants from the air without producing any additional substances. Active air purifiers release charged particles or ions to neutralize pollutants in the air. Hybrid air purifiers combine both passive and active technologies to provide comprehensive air purification. Passive purifiers are generally more affordable and low-maintenance, while active purifiers are more effective at removing smaller particles. Hybrid purifiers offer a balanced approach by combining the benefits of both technologies for optimal air purification.</p></p>
