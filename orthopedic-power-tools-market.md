<p><ul><li>Electric Powered</li><li>Battery Operated</li><li>Pneumatic Powered</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The orthopedic power tools market is categorized into three types: electric powered, battery operated, and pneumatic powered tools. Electric powered tools are connected to an electrical outlet and provide a constant power source. Battery-operated tools offer mobility and flexibility as they are not connected to an external power source. Pneumatic-powered tools use compressed air to function and are commonly used in medical settings. These different types of orthopedic power tools cater to specific requirements and preferences of medical professionals and play a vital role in orthopedic surgeries.</p></p>
