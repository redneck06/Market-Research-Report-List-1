<p><p>The Aircraft Communication System market consists of various types of equipment that enable communication between aircraft and ground stations. Transponders are devices that receive and transmit signals to identify and locate aircraft. Transceivers function as both transmitters and receivers and facilitate two-way communication. Antennas are used to transmit and receive radio signals. Transmitters generate and transmit signals, while receivers capture and process incoming signals. Displays and processors provide information and facilitate control of the communication system. These components collectively form the Aircraft Communication System market.</p></p>
