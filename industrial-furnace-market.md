<p><p>The industrial furnace market can be divided into two main types: combustion-type and electric-type furnaces. Combustion-type furnaces use fuel combustion (such as gas or oil) to generate heat for various industrial processes, including melting, heat treatment, and drying. On the other hand, electric-type furnaces rely on electricity to generate heat, making them more energy-efficient and environmentally friendly. Both types of furnaces have their own advantages and applications, depending on factors like cost, energy efficiency, and specific industrial requirements.</p></p>
