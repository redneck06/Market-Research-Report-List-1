<p><p>Blood irradiation equipment is a medical device used for irradiating blood products in order to prevent graft-versus-host disease (GVHD), transfusion-associated graft-versus-host disease (TAGVHD), and transfusion-related adverse reactions. Blood irradiation is a process that involves exposing blood or blood components to a controlled dose of ionizing radiation to eliminate lymphocytes and other donor T-cells that may cause adverse reactions in the recipient.</p><p>The global blood irradiation equipment market is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years.
