<p><p>The hydrogen gas sensor market consists of various types of sensors to detect the presence of hydrogen gas. Electrochemical sensors use a chemical reaction to generate an electrical current proportional to the gas concentration. Metal oxide semiconductor sensors detect changes in electrical conductivity when hydrogen gas interacts with a metal oxide film. Catalytic sensors rely on a catalytic reaction between the gas and a heated platinum wire to produce a measurable change in resistance. Other types of sensors may include solid-state or thermal conductivity sensors.</p></p>
