<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Hydraulic bender market consists of different types, namely, Torsional Axis Synchronous, Synchronous Machine Liquid, and Electro-Hydraulic Synchronization. In Torsional Axis Synchronous benders, the rotation of the mandrel and the tube is synchronized by a hydraulic motor, ensuring precise bending. Synchronous Machine Liquid benders use fluid as the operating medium to achieve synchronous motion between the mandrel and the tube. Electro-Hydraulic Synchronization benders utilize both hydraulic power and electronic control to achieve accurate synchronization between the mandrel and the tube during bending operations.</p></p>
