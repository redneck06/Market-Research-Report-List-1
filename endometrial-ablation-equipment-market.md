<p><p>Endometrial ablation equipment market includes various types such as Radiofrequency Ablation, Cryoablation, Hydrothermal Ablation, Thermal Balloon, Hysteroscopic Ablation, and Others. Radiofrequency Ablation uses high-frequency electrical energy to destroy the endometrial tissue. Cryoablation involves freezing the tissue to remove the lining of the uterus. Hydrothermal Ablation uses hot water to thermally ablate the endometrial lining. Thermal Balloon involves inserting a balloon filled with heated fluid into the uterus. Hysteroscopic Ablation uses a hysteroscope to visualize and remove the endometrial tissue. Others include various less common techniques for endometrial ablation.</p></p>
