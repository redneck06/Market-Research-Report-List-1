<p><p>Seawater Reverse Osmosis System market is categorized into two types: One-way Reverse Osmosis and Two-way Reverse Osmosis. One-way Reverse Osmosis involves the use of a single membrane to separate salt and impurities from seawater, producing fresh water. This process is suitable for applications where a small amount of fresh water is required. On the other hand, Two-way Reverse Osmosis incorporates multiple stages of membranes, enabling the production of higher volumes of fresh water. This method is more commonly used in larger-scale applications where a significant amount of fresh water is needed.</p></p>
