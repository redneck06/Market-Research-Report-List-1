<p><p>The transportation battery market can be classified into different types based on their chemical compositions. NCM/NCA batteries are made up of nickel, cobalt, manganese, and/or aluminum, offering high energy density. LFP batteries use lithium iron phosphate and are known for their longer lifespan and safety. LCO batteries contain lithium cobalt oxide and are commonly used in consumer electronics due to their high energy density. LMO batteries consist of lithium manganese oxide, providing a balanced combination of energy density and safety. Lastly, there are other types of batteries available in the transportation market, each with their own unique characteristics and applications.</p></p>
