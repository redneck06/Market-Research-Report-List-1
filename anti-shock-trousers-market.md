<p><p>Anti-shock trousers, also known as medical anti-shock garment, have two main market types: military and civil. In the military market, these trousers are primarily used by armed forces for the treatment of hemorrhagic or hypovolemic shock in combat or emergency situations. In the civil market, these trousers are used by emergency medical services, hospitals, and ambulance services to provide immediate care to patients experiencing shock due to trauma, pregnancy complications, or other medical emergencies.</p></p>
