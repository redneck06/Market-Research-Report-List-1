<p><strong>The DLP Chipset Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DMD</li><li>DMD Controller</li><li>DMD Micromirror Drivers</li><li>Evaluation Board</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The DLP (Digital Light Processing) chipset market is divided into several types. The DMD (Digital Micromirror Device) is the core component and includes micro mirrors that reflect light to create images. The DMD controller manages the operation of the DMD, controlling the mirrors' movement to form images. The DMD micromirror drivers drive the individual mirrors on the DMD. Evaluation boards are used for testing and evaluating the performance of DLP chipsets. Each of these components plays a crucial role in the DLP chipset market, enabling advanced projection and display technologies.</p></p>
