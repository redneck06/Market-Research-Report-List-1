<p><p>The Food Freeze-drying Equipment Market mainly consists of two types: Batch Freeze-Drying Equipment and Continuous Freeze-Drying Equipment. Batch Freeze-Drying Equipment involves a process where small batches of food are frozen and dried in a chamber, making it suitable for small-scale production. On the other hand, Continuous Freeze-Drying Equipment is used for large-scale production, where a continuous flow of food is frozen and dried on a conveyor belt. This type of equipment is more efficient and allows for higher production volumes.</p></p>
