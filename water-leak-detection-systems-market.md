<p><p>Water Leak Detection Systems Market Types include passive leak detection systems and active leak detection systems. </p><p>Passive leak detection systems are designed to monitor water flow and detect leaks by measuring changes in pressure, flow rate, or temperature. These systems are typically more cost-effective and easier to install, but they may not provide real-time alerts for immediate action.</p><p>On the other hand, active leak detection systems actively search for leaks by using sensors and alarms. These systems are more advanced and can provide real-time alerts, allowing for quick response and minimizing potential damage. However, they are usually more expensive and require professional installation.</p></p>
