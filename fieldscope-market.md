<p><p>Fieldscope Market Types: Straight Body Fieldscopes and Angled Body Fieldscopes are both optical devices used for observing distant objects in natural settings. Straight Body Fieldscopes have a direct line of sight, providing a more comfortable viewing experience when observing subjects at or near eye level. On the other hand, Angled Body Fieldscopes have an inclined eyepiece, allowing for easier viewing of objects at different heights without straining the neck. These market types cater to different preferences and usage scenarios, providing users with options for their specific observation needs.</p></p>
