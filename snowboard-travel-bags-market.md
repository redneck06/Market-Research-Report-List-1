<p><p>Snowboard travel bags primarily come in two types: hand type and backpack type. Hand type bags are designed with handles and are carried by hand. These bags are usually more compact and lightweight, making them convenient for short trips or carrying minimal gear. On the other hand, backpack type bags have shoulder straps, allowing them to be worn on the back. They are usually larger in size and provide more space for carrying additional gear or personal items. Backpack type bags are preferred by those who need to transport their snowboard more comfortably and for longer distances.</p></p>
