<p><p>A Central Impression Flexo Press (CI Flexo Press) is a type of printing press used for high-volume printing of flexible materials, such as paper, cardboard, and plastic films. It utilizes a central impression cylinder that holds the material to be printed while the other cylinders apply ink to the material. CI Flexo Presses are known for their high print quality, efficiency, and versatility.</p><p>The CI Flexo Press market has been experiencing significant growth in recent years. The increasing demand for flexible packaging materials in various industries, such as food & beverages, pharmaceuticals, and personal care, is one of the key factors driving the market growth.
