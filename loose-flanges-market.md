<p><p>Loose flanges, also known as lap joint flanges, are the type of flanges that are used to connect pipes or tubes together without welding. These flanges are commonly used in low-pressure applications and systems where easy disassembly and maintenance are required. The loose flanges consist of two main components - the stub end and the loose backing flange. The stub end is welded to the pipe or tube, while the backing flange is slid over the pipe and held in place by the pressure of the stub end.</p><p>The loose flanges market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. The increasing demand for flanges from various end-user industries such as oil and gas, chemical, food and beverage, and water treatment is driving the market growth. Loose flanges offer several advantages over other types of flanges, such as easy installation, flexibility, and cost-effectiveness, which further contributes to their growing demand.</p><p>In terms of market outlook, the loose flanges market is expected to continue its growth trajectory.
