<p><p>Optical metrology equipment refers to devices used for measuring and analyzing various properties like dimensions, roughness, and geometry of objects. Contact equipment in this market involves physically touching the object with the measuring device to obtain accurate data. On the other hand, contactless equipment measures objects without physical contact, typically using technologies like laser or light, enabling non-destructive and high-speed measurements. Both types are essential in different industries such as manufacturing, automotive, aerospace, and electronics, providing precise measurements for quality control and process optimization.</p></p>
