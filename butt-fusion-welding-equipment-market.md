<p><p>Butt Fusion Welding Equipment is a type of machinery used to join two pieces of thermoplastic pipes or fittings together. It is a widely adopted welding technique in industries such as oil and gas, water and wastewater treatment, and construction, where the demand for strong and leak-proof joints is essential. The equipment works by melting the ends of thermoplastic pipe or fitting and then pressing them together to form a strong and permanent bond.</p><p>The Butt Fusion Welding Equipment Market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
