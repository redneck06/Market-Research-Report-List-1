<p><p>The elastography system market encompasses various types of technologies used to assess tissue elasticity. Ultrasound elastography utilizes sound waves to measure tissue stiffness and detect abnormalities. Magnetic Resonance Elastography (MRE) employs magnetic resonance imaging to analyze tissue elasticity, providing detailed images of internal structures. Other types of elastography systems may include shear wave elastography and acoustic radiation force impulse imaging, which also utilize ultrasound technology to assess tissue stiffness. These systems play a crucial role in diagnosing conditions like liver fibrosis, breast lesions, and prostate cancer.</p></p>
