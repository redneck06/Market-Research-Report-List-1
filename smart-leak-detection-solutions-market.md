<p><p>Smart leak detection solutions refer to advanced technologies designed to detect and prevent leaks in various settings. The market for these solutions can be categorized into three types: embedded, tethered, and integrated. Embedded solutions are built directly into the infrastructure, such as pipes or tanks, enabling real-time monitoring and rapid response to leaks. Tethered solutions involve using external devices that are connected to the infrastructure for monitoring and leakage detection. Integrated solutions combine various technologies and sensors to monitor and detect leaks, offering a comprehensive approach to leak prevention and management.</p></p>
