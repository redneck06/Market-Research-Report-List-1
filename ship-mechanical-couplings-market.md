<p><strong>The Ship Mechanical Couplings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rigid Ship Mechanical Couplings</li><li>Flexible Ship Mechanical Couplings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ship mechanical couplings are essential components used to connect two rotating shafts in a ship's propulsion system. Rigid ship mechanical couplings are designed to provide a direct and rigid connection between the shafts, ensuring maximum torque transfer and accuracy at high speeds. On the other hand, flexible ship mechanical couplings are engineered with flexing elements to allow for slight misalignment, vibration absorption, and reducing shock loads between the rotating shafts. These couplings provide flexibility and protect the system from wear and tear, making them suitable for applications with varying misalignments and shocks.</p></p>
