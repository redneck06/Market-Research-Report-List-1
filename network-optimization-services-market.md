RAN Optimization focuses on improving the performance and coverage of Radio Access Networks, optimizing signal strength and reducing network congestion for better connectivity. Data Center Optimization focuses on improving efficiency, scalability, and reliability of data centers by streamlining operations, reducing latency, and maximizing resource utilization.</p></p>
