<p><p>The Smart Healthcare Products market encompasses various types of innovative technologies. Electronic Health Record (EHR) refers to digital systems that store patients' medical history and records for efficient access and management. Smart Pills are ingestible capsules containing miniature sensors that transmit real-time data to healthcare professionals, enhancing medication adherence and monitoring. Smart Syringes incorporate safety mechanisms to prevent needlestick injuries and infection transmission. RFID Systems utilize radio frequency identification technology to track and manage medical equipment, supplies, and patient identification, optimizing healthcare operations. These products revolutionize healthcare by improving patient care, safety, and overall operational efficiency.</p></p>
