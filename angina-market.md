<p><p>The angina market refers to the market for medications used in the treatment of angina, a type of chest pain caused by reduced blood flow to the heart. Angina medications can be classified into various types such as beta blockers, calcium antagonists, anticoagulants, antiplatelet drugs, nitrates, ACE inhibitors, and ranolazine. Beta blockers and calcium antagonists help by relaxing and widening blood vessels, while anticoagulants and antiplatelet drugs prevent blood clot formation. Nitrates and ACE inhibitors work to improve blood flow, and ranolazine reduces angina pain by altering the heart's electrical activity.</p></p>
