The Flash market includes NVM IPs that allow data to be electrically erased and reprogrammed. E2PROM market comprises NVM IPs that provide the ability to electronically erase and reprogram data. OTP/MTP market consists of NVM IPs that enable data to be programmed only once or multiple times. The Others market encompasses NVM IPs that do not fit into the mentioned categories, offering different functionalities or features.</p></p>
