<p><p>The HVAC Energy Recovery Ventilator (ERV) market can be classified into two types: the Independent Type and the Integrated Type. The Independent Type ERV is a standalone system that operates separately from the HVAC system, providing independent fresh air ventilation and energy recovery functions. On the other hand, the Integrated Type ERV is a built-in component of the HVAC system, combining the functions of both ventilation and energy recovery within the same unit. These two types cater to different needs and preferences of customers in the HVAC ERV market.</p></p>
