<p><strong>The IgE Allergy Blood Test Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ELISA Testing</li><li>Fluorescent Enzyme Immunoassays (FEIA) Testing</li><li>Chemiluminescent Immunoassays Testing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IgE Allergy Blood Test market is categorized into various types based on the methods used for testing. ELISA Testing is a widely used technique that detects specific IgE antibodies in the blood by utilizing an enzyme-linked immunosorbent assay. Fluorescent Enzyme Immunoassays (FEIA) Testing employs fluorescence-labeled antibodies to detect IgE antibodies, providing rapid and accurate results. Chemiluminescent Immunoassays Testing uses a light-emitting reaction to detect IgE antibodies, allowing for sensitive and precise measurements. These testing methods play a crucial role in diagnosing allergies and determining appropriate treatment options.</p></p>
