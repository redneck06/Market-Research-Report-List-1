<p><p>The Smart Power Management market includes various types of solutions to effectively manage power consumption. Voltage regulation involves maintaining a stable voltage level to prevent damage to devices or appliances. Current limiting restricts the flow of excessive current, safeguarding devices from overheating and potential failures. Load distribution ensures an equal distribution of power across multiple devices, preventing overloading in specific areas. Other market types may include energy monitoring systems, power factor correction, or demand response solutions. These technologies collectively enable efficient and optimized power usage in various sectors and applications.</p></p>
