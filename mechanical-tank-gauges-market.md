<p><p>Mechanical tank gauges are devices used to measure the level of liquid in tanks. There are various types of mechanical tank gauges available in the market. Tubular type gauges consist of a series of transparent tubes that display the liquid level. Reflection type gauges use mirrors to reflect the liquid level. Transparent type gauges have a transparent glass or plastic tube to view the liquid level directly. Bicolor type gauges use two different colored liquid chambers to indicate the level. Other types include magnetic, float, and swiveling gauges, each utilizing different mechanisms to measure and display the liquid level in tanks.</p></p>
