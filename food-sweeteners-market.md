<p><p>The food sweeteners market comprises various types of sweeteners such as aspartame, acesulfame-K, saccharin, sucralose, neotame, stevia, and others. Aspartame is a low-calorie sweetener commonly used in beverages and desserts. Acesulfame-K is a high-intensity sweetener used in a wide range of food products. Saccharin is an artificial sweetener that is often used in diet foods. Sucralose is a zero-calorie sweetener that is heat-stable and commonly found in baked goods. Neotame is a newer sweetener that is sweeter than aspartame and often used in processed foods. Stevia is a natural sweetener derived from the Stevia rebaudiana plant and used in various food and beverage products. Other sweeteners include sugar alcohols like sorbitol and xylitol.</p></p>
