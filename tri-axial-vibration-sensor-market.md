<p><ul><li>Accelerometers</li><li>Velocity Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tri-axial vibration sensor market includes accelerometers and velocity sensors. Accelerometers measure vibration in terms of acceleration, providing detailed information on magnitude and frequency of vibrations. They are used in various applications such as machinery monitoring, automotive testing, and aerospace industries. Velocity sensors, on the other hand, measure vibrations in terms of velocity, giving a broader understanding of overall vibration levels. These sensors are commonly used in applications such as structural health monitoring, building diagnostics, and condition monitoring of rotating equipment. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in analyzing and mitigating vibration-related issues in different industries.</p></p>
