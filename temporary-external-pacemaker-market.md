<p><strong>The Temporary External Pacemaker Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Chamber Temporary Pacemaker</li><li>Dual Chamber Temporary Pacemaker</li><li>Triple Chamber Temporary Pacemaker</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The market for temporary external pacemakers can be categorized into three types: Single Chamber, Dual Chamber, and Triple Chamber. Single Chamber pacemakers are designed to deliver electrical signals to one chamber of the heart, usually the ventricles. Dual Chamber pacemakers deliver signals to both the atria and ventricles, allowing for more synchronized heartbeats. Triple Chamber pacemakers are the most advanced, as they deliver signals to both atria and ventricles, as well as coordinate electrical activity between the two. These different types cater to patients with varying heart conditions and requirements.</p></p>
