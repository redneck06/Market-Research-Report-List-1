<p><p>The market for galvanic isolated drivers can be divided into two main types: IGBT driver market and MOSFET driver market. IGBT drivers are used for controlling and driving insulated gate bipolar transistors, which are commonly used in high-power applications. On the other hand, MOSFET drivers are used for driving metal-oxide-semiconductor field-effect transistors, which are typically used for low-power applications. Both types of drivers provide galvanic isolation, which ensures electrical safety and reduces noise interference in various electronic systems.</p></p>
