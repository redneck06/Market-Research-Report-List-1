<p><p>Tourniquets are medical devices used to restrict blood flow in limbs during surgical procedures or to draw blood for testing. There are two main types of tourniquets: latex and TPE (Thermoplastic Elastomer). Latex tourniquets are made from natural rubber and offer good flexibility and durability. TPE tourniquets are made from a synthetic rubber-like material and provide similar functionality to latex tourniquets but are latex-free, making them suitable for individuals with latex allergies. Both types are commonly used in healthcare settings for their effectiveness in controlling blood flow.</p></p>
