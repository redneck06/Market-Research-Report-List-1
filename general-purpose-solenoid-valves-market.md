<p><strong>The General Purpose Solenoid Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Acting</li><li>Semi-direct Acting</li><li>Pilot Operated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>General Purpose Solenoid Valves are used to control the flow of liquids or gases in various industrial applications. There are three types in this market: </p><p>1. Direct Acting: These valves operate directly with the help of an electromagnetic coil. They are simple and compact, suitable for small flow rates.</p><p>2. Semi-Direct Acting: These valves have a small pilot valve and a larger main valve. The pilot valve controls the flow of media to open or close the main valve.</p><p>3. Pilot Operated: These valves use a pilot valve to control the opening and closing of the main valve. They are capable of handling higher flow rates and pressure differentials.</p></p>
