<p><strong>The Automatic Laser Plotter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>External Drum Laser Photoplotters</li><li>Internal Drum Laser Photoplotters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Laser Plotter market consists of two main types: External Drum Laser Photoplotters and Internal Drum Laser Photoplotters. External Drum Laser Photoplotters utilize a rotating drum outside the plotter, onto which the image is projected and then transferred onto the film or substrate. On the other hand, Internal Drum Laser Photoplotters have the rotating drum located inside the plotter, enabling direct writing onto the film or substrate. Both market types offer automated laser plotting capabilities but differ in the positioning of the drum, affecting the plotting process.</p></p>
