<p><p>The Radio Frequency Dryer market consists of two main types: Continuous Radio Frequency Dryer and Intermittent Radio Frequency Dryer. Continuous RF Dryers are designed to continuously process large quantities of materials by utilizing a conveyor belt system. They are ideal for industries requiring high-volume and uninterrupted drying operations. On the other hand, Intermittent RF Dryers are suitable for applications that involve smaller batches or different drying processes. They can be manually loaded and unloaded, making them flexible for various production needs. Both types offer efficient and effective drying solutions, catering to different industry requirements.</p></p>
