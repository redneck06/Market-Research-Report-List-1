Increasing awareness about the harmful effects of plastic waste on wildlife and ecosystems has led to various initiatives advocating for the reduction or elimination of single-use plastic straws. As a result, there has been a significant market demand for sustainable alternatives such as paper straws, bamboo straws, or reusable metal straws.</p><p>Furthermore, strict regulations imposed by governments and bans on single-use plastics in certain regions have propelled the market for eco-friendly alternatives.
