<p><p>Navigation lighting refers to the lights used on ships, aircraft, and other vehicles to ensure safe navigation during low visibility conditions, such as at night or in adverse weather. The increasing demand for maritime trade and shipping activities, as well as the rising adoption of air travel, are driving the growth of this market. Moreover, the strict regulations imposed by governing bodies, such as the International Maritime Organization (IMO) and the Federal Aviation Administration (FAA), regarding the installation of navigation lights, are further fueling the market growth.</p><p>The market is witnessing a shift towards LED (Light Emitting Diode) lights due to their numerous advantages over traditional lighting systems. LED lights offer energy efficiency, longer lifespan, lower maintenance costs, and improved visibility.
