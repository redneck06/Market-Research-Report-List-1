<p><ul><li>SPDT switch</li><li>Double pole double throw switch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RF PXI Switch market consists of different types of switches, such as the SPDT switch and the Double Pole Double Throw (DPDT) switch. SPDT stands for Single Pole Double Throw, meaning it has one input and two outputs that can switch between two different paths. On the other hand, DPDT stands for Double Pole Double Throw, which has two inputs and two outputs, allowing for switching between two separate circuits simultaneously. These switches are commonly used in RF PXI systems for signal routing and management applications.</p></p>
