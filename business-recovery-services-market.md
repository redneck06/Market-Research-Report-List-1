<p><p>Business Recovery Services market types include administrative takeover, compulsory liquidation, creditor voluntary liquidation, voluntary management, and others. Administrative takeover refers to the process where a professional is appointed to take control of a financially distressed company's operations. Compulsory liquidation occurs when a court orders the closure and liquidation of a company. Creditor voluntary liquidation refers to a situation where the company's creditors initiate the process of liquidation. Voluntary management involves appointing an external professional to manage the company's affairs. Other types in this market may include debt restructuring, turnaround management, and insolvency advice services.</p></p>
