<p><p>Non-contact vein illumination devices are medical devices used for identifying and locating veins in a patient's body. The market is driven by several factors, including the rising prevalence of chronic diseases such as diabetes and obesity, the increasing number of surgical procedures, and the growing geriatric population. Vein visualization devices help in reducing the time required for venous access and minimize patient discomfort during medical procedures.</p><p>Moreover, advancements in technology, such as the development of portable and compact devices, have expanded the applications of non-contact vein illumination devices, further propelling the market growth.
