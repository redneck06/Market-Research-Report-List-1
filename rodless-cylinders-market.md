<p><p>Rodless cylinders are a type of pneumatic or hydraulic actuator used in various industries. Single-acting cylinders have one inlet for fluid pressure to extend the piston, while an internal spring retracts it. Double-acting cylinders have two inlets for fluid pressure to extend or retract the piston. Single-acting cylinders are commonly used where weight is a concern, while double-acting cylinders offer more control over movement and can exert force in both directions. Both types offer efficient and compact solutions for linear motion applications in industrial automation.</p></p>
