<p><p>Remote Deposit Capture (RDC) is a technology that enables bank customers to scan and electronically transmit checks from their remote locations to the bank for deposit. It eliminates the need for customers to physically visit a bank branch to deposit the check. Instead, customers can scan the check using a secure scanner and transmit the images to the bank using a computer or mobile device. RDC has gained significant popularity in recent years due to its convenience, cost savings, and time efficiency.</p><p>The Remote Deposit Capture market has witnessed substantial growth in recent years and is expected to continue its upward trajectory.
