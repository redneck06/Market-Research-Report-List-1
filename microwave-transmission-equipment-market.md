<p><p>Microwave transmission equipment market includes two main types: Full Indoor and Full Outdoor systems. Full Indoor equipment refers to microwave systems that are installed indoors, typically within a building or data center. These systems require additional infrastructure, such as antennas and cables, for outdoor connectivity. Alternatively, Full Outdoor equipment is designed to operate outdoors without the need for any additional indoor infrastructure. These systems are commonly used in rural areas or remote locations where it may be challenging to establish indoor installations. Both types are crucial components for establishing reliable microwave communication networks.</p></p>
