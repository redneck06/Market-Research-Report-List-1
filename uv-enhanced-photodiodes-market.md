<p><strong>The UV Enhanced Photodiodes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inversion Channel Series</li><li>Planar Diffused Series</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV Enhanced Photodiodes market can be categorized into two types - Inversion Channel Series and Planar Diffused Series. Inversion Channel Series photodiodes are composed of a P-N junction and exhibit low dark current and high responsivity. They are suitable for applications requiring high-speed response and low power consumption. On the other hand, Planar Diffused Series photodiodes utilize a P-N integrated structure and offer high sensitivity and low noise performance. They are commonly used in applications such as flame detection and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
