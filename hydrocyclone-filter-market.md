<p><p>A hydrocyclone filter is a device used for the separation of particles from a fluid based on their size and shape. It consists of a cylindrical chamber with a conical bottom, where the fluid enters tangentially, creating a spinning motion. This spinning motion causes the particles to be forced to the outer edges of the chamber, while the clean fluid moves towards the center and exits through an overflow pipe. The separated particles are collected at the bottom and discharged as a waste stream.</p><p>The hydrocyclone filter market is expected to witness substantial growth in the coming years.
