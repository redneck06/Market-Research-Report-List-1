<p><p>The Single Girder Bridge Crane market includes different types of cranes, such as Gantry Crane and Half-Gantry Crane. A Gantry Crane is a type of overhead crane with a single beam that is supported by legs on both sides. It is commonly used in outdoor applications for lifting heavy loads. On the other hand, a Half-Gantry Crane is similar to a Gantry Crane but is supported by legs on only one side, making it suitable for indoor use near walls or columns. Both types of cranes provide efficient and reliable solutions for material handling requirements.</p></p>
