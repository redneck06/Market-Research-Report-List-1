Electric patio heaters are powered by electricity and provide instant heat without any fuel emissions. Natural gas patio heaters are connected to a main gas supply line and offer a steady source of heat, suitable for permanent installations. Propane patio heaters use propane gas tanks to generate heat and are portable, making them ideal for outdoor spaces where mobility is required. Each type has its own advantages and considerations, allowing consumers to choose the most suitable option for their needs.</p></p>
