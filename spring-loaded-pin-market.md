<p><strong>The Spring-Loaded Pin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Mounted Type</li><li>Through Hole Type</li><li>Floating Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spring-loaded pins are commonly used in electronic devices to establish reliable electrical connections. They are available in different market types, including surface-mounted, through-hole, and floating types. </p><p>Surface-mounted spring-loaded pins are designed to be soldered onto the surface of a printed circuit board (PCB), providing a secure and compact connection.</p><p>Through-hole spring-loaded pins, on the other hand, are inserted through drilled holes in the PCB and soldered, offering more stability and durability.</p><p>Floating type spring-loaded pins allow for flexibility and movement, making them suitable for applications with misalignment or non-perpendicular mating surfaces. They ensure continuous electrical contact even in challenging conditions.</p></p>
