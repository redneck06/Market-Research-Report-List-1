<p><p>The underwater remotely operated vehicles (ROVs) market can be categorized into three types: Micro ROVs, Lightwork-class ROVs, and Heavywork-class ROVs. Micro ROVs are compact and lightweight, primarily used for inspection and research purposes in shallow waters. Lightwork-class ROVs are slightly larger and more powerful, used for tasks like pipeline inspection and maintenance. Heavywork-class ROVs are the largest and strongest, capable of operating at greater depths and performing complex tasks such as deep-sea exploration and offshore construction. These ROVs cater to different needs and requirements in the underwater industry.</p></p>
