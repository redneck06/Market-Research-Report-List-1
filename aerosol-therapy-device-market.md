<p><strong>The Aerosol Therapy Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Metered Dose Inhalers (MDIs)</li><li>Dry Powder Inhalers (DPIs)</li><li>Nebulizers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Aerosol Therapy Device market consists of three types: Metered Dose Inhalers (MDIs), Dry Powder Inhalers (DPIs), and Nebulizers. MDIs deliver medication through a pressurized canister, releasing a precise dose with each inhalation. DPIs require the user to inhale powdered medication directly from a device, without the need for propellants. Nebulizers, on the other hand, convert liquid medication into a fine mist that can be inhaled through a mask or mouthpiece. These devices provide varying methods to deliver medication to individuals with respiratory conditions such as asthma and COPD.</p></p>
