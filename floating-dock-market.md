The increasing demand for water recreational activities and the growing number of waterfront properties are driving the market growth. Floating docks offer flexibility, easy installation, and durability, which makes them an ideal choice for both commercial and residential applications. Additionally, the rising investment in the marine industry, especially in developing countries, is further fueling the market growth.</p><p>Moreover, the latest trends in the floating dock market include the adoption of eco-friendly materials and technologies for dock construction.
