<p><strong>The Automated Sortation System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear Sortation Systems</li><li>Loop Sortation Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automated Sortation System market can be categorized into two main types: Linear Sortation Systems and Loop Sortation Systems. Linear Sortation Systems utilize a linear conveyor belt to transport and sort items in a single line, enabling high-speed and accurate sorting. On the other hand, Loop Sortation Systems utilize a closed-loop design with multiple conveyor belts arranged in a loop, allowing for continuous sorting with minimal stops. Both systems play a crucial role in optimizing logistics and supply chain operations by efficiently organizing and distributing goods.</p></p>
