<p><p>Dust mops can be classified into two main types: ordinary dust mops and electrostatic dust mops. Ordinary dust mops typically consist of a piece of fabric or yarn attached to a handle. They are designed to pick up and trap dust and debris on floors and other surfaces. On the other hand, electrostatic dust mops use static electricity to attract and collect dust particles without the need for wetting agents or chemicals. This technology makes them more effective in capturing fine dust and hairs, providing a deeper cleaning experience.</p></p>
