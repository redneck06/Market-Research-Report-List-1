<p><p>Epitope tags are small protein sequences that are fused to target proteins, aiding their detection and purification. V5, c-Myc, HA, GFP, RFP, MBP, GST, and Others are popular types of epitope tags used in research. V5, c-Myc, and HA tags are commonly used for immunoprecipitation and protein localization studies. GFP and RFP tags enable visualization of tagged proteins under fluorescence microscopy. MBP and GST tags are employed for protein purification. "Others" refers to various additional epitope tags like FLAG, His, or Strep, chosen based on specific experimental needs. These epitope tags facilitate diverse applications in protein analysis and investigation.</p></p>
