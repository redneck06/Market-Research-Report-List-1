<p><p>The Portable Metal Detectors market consists of various types, including Pulse Induction (PI), Beat Frequency Oscillations (BFO), and others. Pulse Induction metal detectors use short bursts of current to produce magnetic fields, detecting metal objects through analyzing the decay of the magnetic field. Beat Frequency Oscillations detectors rely on two oscillators, with one fixed and the other adjustable, detecting metal by analyzing changes in the beat frequency created. Other types of portable metal detectors may include different technologies and mechanisms for metal detection.</p></p>
