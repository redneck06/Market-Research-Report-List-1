<p><p>On-premises digital banking platforms are hosted and operated within an organization's own infrastructure, enabling complete control over data security and customization but requiring significant upfront investments and maintenance costs. Conversely, cloud-based digital banking platforms are hosted on remote servers and accessed via the internet, providing scalability, flexibility, and cost-efficiency, but relying on external providers for security and updates. Both types strive to offer advanced banking services online, but the choice between on-premises and cloud solutions depends on factors like budget, data sensitivity, and scalability requirements.</p></p>
