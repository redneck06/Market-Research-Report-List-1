<p><p>The aesthetic lasers market can be classified into three main types: gas lasers, solid lasers, and semiconductor lasers. Gas lasers use a gaseous medium, such as carbon dioxide or argon, to produce the laser beam. Solid lasers use a solid medium, such as a crystal or glass, to generate the laser beam. Semiconductor lasers, on the other hand, use semiconductor materials to create the laser beam. Each type of laser has its own unique properties and applications in the field of aesthetic treatments.</p></p>
