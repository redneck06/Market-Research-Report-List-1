<p><strong>The CO2 Laser Tube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CO2 Glass Laser Tube</li><li>CO2 Radio Frequency Laser Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CO2 laser tube market consists of two main types: CO2 glass laser tube and CO2 radio frequency laser tube. CO2 glass laser tubes use a mixture of CO2, nitrogen, and helium gases to generate laser beams, making them commonly used in cutting, engraving, and marking applications. On the other hand, CO2 radio frequency laser tubes use radio frequency energy to excite the CO2 gas and produce laser beams, which are suitable for high-power industrial applications such as material processing, welding, and drilling.</p></p>
