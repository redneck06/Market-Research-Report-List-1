<p><strong>The Dental Turbine Handpiece Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inside Exhaust Handpiece</li><li>Outside Exhaust Handpiece</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dental Turbine Handpieces are available in two main types: inside exhaust handpieces and outside exhaust handpieces. Inside exhaust handpieces have an integrated exhaust system within the handpiece itself, allowing air and water to be directed out through the back of the handpiece. On the other hand, outside exhaust handpieces have a separate external exhaust system, typically attached to the dental unit or dental delivery system. Both types serve the same purpose of providing fast and efficient cutting and grinding of teeth, but differ in terms of how the exhaust is managed.</p></p>
