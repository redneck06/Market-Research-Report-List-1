<p><p>The plating services market includes various types of plating techniques. Cadmium plating is a process where a thin layer of cadmium metal is applied to the surface of an object to enhance corrosion resistance. Nickel plating involves depositing a layer of nickel onto a surface, which provides protection against wear and corrosion. Zinc plating is the application of a zinc coating on objects to prevent corrosion. Copper plating refers to the process of adding a layer of copper onto a surface for improved conductivity. Other types of plating services include gold, silver, and chrome plating, which provide unique aesthetic qualities or specialized functional properties.</p></p>
