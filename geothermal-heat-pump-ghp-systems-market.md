<p><p>Geothermal Heat Pump (GHP) Systems, also known as ground source heat pump systems, are a type of HVAC (heating, ventilation, and air conditioning) technology that uses the natural heat stored in the earth to provide heating, cooling, and hot water for residential and commercial buildings. These systems utilize underground pipes and a heat pump unit to extract heat from the ground during winter and deposit excess heat into the ground during summer, providing an energy-efficient and sustainable alternative to traditional heating and cooling systems.</p><p>The Geothermal Heat Pump (GHP) Systems Market is witnessing significant growth due to several factors. Firstly, governments worldwide are increasingly promoting the adoption of renewable energy technologies to reduce greenhouse gas emissions and combat climate change.
