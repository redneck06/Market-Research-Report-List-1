<p><strong>The Liquid Cooling Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Liquid Heat Exchanger System</li><li>Compressor-based System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Liquid cooling systems are classified into two main types: liquid heat exchanger systems and compressor-based systems. </p><p>Liquid heat exchanger systems use a liquid to transfer heat away from the components, such as computer processors or industrial machinery. The liquid absorbs the heat and then passes through a heat exchanger, where it dissipates the heat into the surrounding environment. These systems are commonly used in data centers and high-performance computing applications.</p><p>Compressor-based systems, on the other hand, utilize a refrigerant to cool the components. The refrigerant absorbs the heat and then undergoes a compression process to release the heat to the ambient air. These systems are mainly employed in residential and commercial air conditioning units, refrigeration systems, and some industrial applications.</p></p>
