<p><p>A hemostatic pad is a medical device used to control bleeding by promoting blood clotting at the site of injury. Hemostatic pads are made from materials that contain specific clotting agents, such as thrombin or fibrin, that help accelerate the blood clotting process.</p><p>The hemostatic pad market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. <p><strong>The Hemostatic Pad Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gelatin Hemostatic Pad</li><li>Fibrin Hemostatic Pad</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Hemostatic Pad market consists of various types, including Gelatin Hemostatic Pads and Fibrin Hemostatic Pads. Gelatin Hemostatic Pads are made from animal collagen and work by absorbing blood and forming a clot to stop bleeding. Fibrin Hemostatic Pads, on the other hand, are made from human or animal blood and contain fibrinogen, a protein that helps in clot formation. Both types serve the purpose of promoting hemostasis, but differ in their composition and mechanism of action.</p></p>
