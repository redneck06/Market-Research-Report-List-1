<p><strong>The Pharmaceutical Grade Glucose Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>API Grade</li><li>Excipient Grade</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Pharmaceutical Grade Glucose market can be categorized into two types: API Grade and Excipient Grade. API Grade refers to the glucose that is used as an Active Pharmaceutical Ingredient in the formulation of medicinal products. It must meet strict quality standards and purity requirements for therapeutic efficacy. Excipient Grade, on the other hand, denotes glucose that is used as an inert substance in pharmaceutical formulations to aid in the manufacturing, stability, or administration of drugs. It must also conform to specific quality criteria but is not intended to have any therapeutic effect itself.</p></p>
