<p><p>Visual Nurse Call Systems are used in healthcare facilities to enable patients to call for assistance from caregivers. There are two main types of Visual Nurse Call Systems: Conventional and Smart Systems. </p><p>Conventional Systems include visual indicators and audible alarms to alert caregivers of patients' calls. They generally have limited functionality and are more cost-effective but may lack advanced features.</p><p>On the other hand, Smart Systems utilize advanced technologies like wireless communication, touchscreens, and integration with patient monitoring systems. They offer additional features like two-way communication, patient data tracking, and automated workflows, enhancing efficiency and patient care. However, Smart Systems tend to be more expensive.</p></p>
