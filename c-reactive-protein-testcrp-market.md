<p><p>The C-Reactive Protein Test (CRP) is a blood test that measures the level of C-reactive protein in the body. It is also used to evaluate the response to treatment and to assess the risk of developing cardiovascular events.</p><p>The global C-Reactive Protein Test (CRP) market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.00% during the forecast period.
