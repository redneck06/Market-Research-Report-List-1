<p><p>Tonsil scissors are commonly used in medical procedures to remove tonsils. They come in two main types: serrated and non-serrated. Serrated tonsil scissors have a notched blade edge, which provides a better grip on the tonsils for more efficient cutting. This type of scissors is often favored for its ability to securely hold the tissue during surgery. On the other hand, non-serrated tonsil scissors have a smooth, straight blade edge without any notches. These scissors are generally preferred for delicate procedures where a clean, precise cut is required.</p></p>
