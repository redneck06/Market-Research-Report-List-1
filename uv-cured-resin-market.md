<p><strong>The UV Cured Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oligomers</li><li>Monomers</li><li>Photoinitiators</li><li>Additives</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV Cured Resin market consists of four main types: Oligomers, Monomers, Photoinitiators, and Additives. Oligomers are prepolymers that determine the final properties of the cured resin, such as flexibility and hardness. Monomers are small molecules that undergo polymerization to form the backbone of the cured resin. Photoinitiators are compounds that initiate the curing process when exposed to UV light. Lastly, Additives are substances added to the resin formulation to modify its physical properties, such as color, viscosity, or durability. These different types work together to create a versatile and effective UV cured resin product.</p></p>
