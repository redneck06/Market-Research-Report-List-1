<p><strong>The Retroreflective Tapes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Glass Bead Type</li><li>Micro Prismatic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Retroreflective tapes are commonly classified into two main types: Glass Bead Type and Micro Prismatic Type. Glass Bead Type tapes utilize spherical glass beads to reflect light back towards its source, providing visibility in low light conditions. On the other hand, Micro Prismatic Type tapes use tiny prisms to achieve a similar effect. Both types of tapes are widely used for enhancing visibility and safety in various applications such as road signs, vehicle markings, and personal protective equipment.</p></p>
