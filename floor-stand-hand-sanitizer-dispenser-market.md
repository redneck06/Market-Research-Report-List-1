<p><p>The floor stand hand sanitizer dispenser market can be classified into two main types: contact and non-contact. Contact dispensers require physical contact with the dispenser to activate the hand sanitizer, typically through pressing a button or lever. Non-contact dispensers, on the other hand, utilize sensor technology to detect the presence of hands and dispense sanitizer without the need for any physical contact. Both types provide a convenient and hygienic solution for promoting hand sanitization in public spaces, with non-contact dispensers offering an added layer of cleanliness by minimizing potential surface contamination.</p></p>
