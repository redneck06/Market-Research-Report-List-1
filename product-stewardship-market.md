<p><p>Product Stewardship refers to the approach of managing the environmental, health, and safety impacts of a product throughout its lifecycle. It involves the responsible management of products from their design and manufacturing to their use, disposal, and recycling. The aim of product stewardship is to ensure that products are safe for consumers, workers, and the environment, and to minimize the negative impacts associated with their production and use.</p><p>The Product Stewardship Market is witnessing significant growth due to various factors. Firstly, there is an increasing focus on sustainability and environmental regulations across the globe. Governments and regulatory bodies are implementing stringent regulations to encourage manufacturers to adopt responsible product stewardship practices.</p><p>Secondly, consumers are becoming more conscious of the environmental and health impacts of the products they use. This has led to a growing demand for products that are environmentally friendly and safe, driving manufacturers to adopt product stewardship practices.</p><p>Moreover, the rising adoption of circular economy principles is also driving the growth of the product stewardship market. Companies are increasingly looking for ways to extend the life cycle of their products through recycling, reuse, and remanufacturing, thereby minimizing waste and resource consumption.</p><p>Furthermore, technological advancements such as the Internet of Things (IoT) and blockchain are expected to play a significant role in enhancing product stewardship practices.
