<p><p>The automatic parking robots market encompasses various types of technologies that assist in parking vehicles without human intervention. Magnetic guided systems utilize magnets embedded in the ground to guide the robots to the designated parking spots accurately. Laser guided systems rely on laser sensors to create a virtual map of the parking area and guide the robots accordingly. Apart from these two commonly used types, other methods like ultrasonic sensors or computer vision-based systems are also employed in automatic parking robots to achieve efficient and precise parking operations.</p></p>
