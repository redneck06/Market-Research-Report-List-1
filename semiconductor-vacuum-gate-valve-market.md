<p><p>The semiconductor vacuum gate valve market offers two types of valves: pneumatic and manual. Pneumatic valves are operated by compressed air, allowing for automated and remote control of vacuum flow. These valves are preferred in industrial settings for their efficiency and precision. On the other hand, manual valves are manually operated using a handle or a handwheel, making them suitable for smaller applications that require direct human control. These valves are typically used in research laboratories or smaller-scale operations where manual adjustment is preferred over automation.</p></p>
