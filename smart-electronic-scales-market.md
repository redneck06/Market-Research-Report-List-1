<p><p>Smart electronic scales are available in two main types based on their connectivity options: Wi-Fi connection and Bluetooth connection. Wi-Fi-enabled scales allow users to connect the device to their home network, enabling them to sync their weight and body composition data with their smartphones or other devices from anywhere within the Wi-Fi range. On the other hand, Bluetooth-enabled scales connect directly to a user's smartphone or tablet using Bluetooth technology, eliminating the need for a Wi-Fi network. Both types offer convenient and seamless data tracking, allowing users to monitor their health and wellness progress effortlessly.</p></p>
