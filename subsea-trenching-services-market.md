<p><strong>The Subsea Trenching Services Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Trenchers</li><li>Jet Trenchers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The subsea trenching services market offers various types of trenching techniques, including mechanical trenchers and jet trenchers. Mechanical trenchers utilize cutting or digging tools to excavate the seabed and create trenches. These tools are typically attached to remotely operated vehicles (ROVs) for precision and accuracy. On the other hand, jet trenchers use high-pressure water jets to remove sediment and create the required trenches. Both types of trenchers have their own advantages and are employed based on factors such as soil conditions and project requirements.</p></p>
