<p><p>The Thermal Infrared Sensor market consists of two main types: Photon Detection and Thermal Detection. Photon Detection sensors work by detecting individual photons of infrared radiation, converting them into an electrical signal. These sensors are highly sensitive and commonly used in applications such as medical imaging and surveillance systems. On the other hand, Thermal Detection sensors measure the changes in temperature to detect infrared radiation. These sensors are commonly used in applications like industrial monitoring, fire detection, and thermal imaging cameras. Both types play crucial roles in various industries, depending on their specific capabilities and applications.</p></p>
