<p><p>The Helium Booster market can be broadly categorized into two types: Pneumatic and Electric. Pneumatic Helium Boosters utilize air pressure to increase the helium gas pressure. They typically have a higher flow rate and are often used in applications where a continuous supply of helium is required. On the other hand, Electric Helium Boosters use an electrical power source to generate the necessary pressure increase. They are often more compact and suitable for smaller-scale applications. Both types cater to different needs and requirements in the helium gas industry.</p></p>
