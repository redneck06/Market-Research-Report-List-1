<p><strong>The Personal Radiation Monitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Type</li><li>Passive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Personal Radiation Monitor market is divided into two types: Active Type and Passive Type. Active Type monitors continuously measure and alert individuals about the radiation levels in their environment. They are equipped with sensors that provide real-time readings and alarms. On the other hand, Passive Type monitors do not have real-time monitoring capability, but they capture and store radiation data over a period of time which can be analyzed later. These types of monitors are often used for retrospective analysis and research purposes.</p></p>
