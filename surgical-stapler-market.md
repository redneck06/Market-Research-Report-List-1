<p><p>The Surgical Stapler market comprises two main types: Laparoscopic Stapler and Open Stapler. Laparoscopic Stapler is a device used in minimally invasive surgeries, where small incisions are made and a camera is inserted for visualization. This stapler is specially designed for precise and efficient stapling in tight spaces. On the other hand, Open Stapler is used in traditional open surgeries, where a large incision is made. It has a different design and functionality to accommodate the needs of open surgical procedures. Both types serve the purpose of securely sealing and connecting tissues during surgeries.</p></p>
