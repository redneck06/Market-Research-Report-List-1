<p><p>The Benchtop Hydrogen Gas Generator market consists of two types: Traditional Alkaline Electrolyzer and PEM Electrolyzer. Traditional Alkaline Electrolyzer relies on a liquid electrolyte solution to split water molecules into hydrogen and oxygen gases. It has been widely used for decades due to its low cost and efficiency. On the other hand, PEM Electrolyzer utilizes a polymer electrolyte membrane to generate hydrogen gas. This type of generator is known for its compact size, high purity output, and quick response time, making it ideal for on-site and portable applications.</p></p>
