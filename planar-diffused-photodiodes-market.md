<p><p>Planar diffused photodiodes are widely used in various industries for light detection and sensing applications. The three main types of planar diffused photodiodes are PN type, PIN type, and Avalanche type. </p><p>PN type photodiodes have a P-N junction, where the light-generated electrons and holes are collected, generating a photocurrent. PIN type photodiodes have an intrinsic layer between the P and N regions, which reduces capacitance and increases the speed of response. Avalanche type photodiodes have a higher voltage applied across the diode, causing the multiplication of photocurrent.</p><p>There are also other types of planar diffused photodiodes available in the market that cater to specific requirements and specialized applications.</p></p>
