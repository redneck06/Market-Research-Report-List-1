<p><p>The aluminum foil heater market consists of two types: single layer aluminum foil hot melt type and double layer aluminum foil paste type. The single layer aluminum foil hot melt type is made of a single layer of aluminum foil with a hot melt adhesive applied to one side. It is commonly used for small-scale applications and offers a simple installation process. On the other hand, the double layer aluminum foil paste type consists of two layers of aluminum foil with a paste adhesive applied between them. This type is often used for larger-scale applications and provides enhanced durability and heat conductivity.</p></p>
