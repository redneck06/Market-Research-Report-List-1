<p><p>The internal audit software market offers two main types of solutions: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based software operates on remote servers, enabling users to access it via the internet. It eliminates the need for physical infrastructure and provides flexible scalability. On the other hand, on-premises software is installed and hosted locally, requiring dedicated hardware and IT resources. While it offers more control and data security, it requires higher upfront costs and ongoing maintenance. Both types provide organizations with effective tools to streamline internal audit processes and enhance efficiency.</p></p>
