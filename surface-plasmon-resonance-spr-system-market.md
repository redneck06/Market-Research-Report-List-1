<p><p>Surface Plasmon Resonance (SPR) System is a label-free optical technique used for real-time monitoring of biomolecular interactions. It is widely used in the fields of drug discovery, proteomics, and life sciences research. The system works by measuring the refractive index changes near the surface of a metal film when biomolecules bind to it. These changes provide valuable information about the affinity, kinetics, and specificity of the biomolecular interactions.</p><p>The Surface Plasmon Resonance (SPR) System Market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
