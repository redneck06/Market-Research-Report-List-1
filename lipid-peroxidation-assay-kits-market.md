<p><p>Lipid peroxidation assay kits are used to measure the level of lipid peroxidation, an oxidative degradation of lipids, in various samples. The market for these assay kits can be classified into two types: colorimetric and fluorometric. Colorimetric assays rely on the change in color intensity to determine the level of lipid peroxidation, while fluorometric assays measure the fluorescence emitted by the oxidized lipids. Both types offer different advantages and limitations, allowing researchers to choose the most suitable method for their specific needs.</p></p>
