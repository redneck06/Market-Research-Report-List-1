<p><p>The Biophysical Environmental Assessments market includes two main types - Terrestrial Assessments and Aquatic Assessments. Terrestrial Assessments focus on evaluating the impact of human activities on land-based ecosystems, such as forests, fields, and urban areas. These assessments analyze factors like biodiversity, soil quality, and air pollution. On the other hand, Aquatic Assessments focus on evaluating the impact of human activities on water-based ecosystems, including lakes, rivers, and oceans. These assessments analyze factors like water quality, habitat degradation, and species abundance. Both types of assessments help in understanding and mitigating the environmental consequences of human actions.</p></p>
