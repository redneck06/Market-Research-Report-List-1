<p><p>The ship davits market can be categorized into three types: hydraulic, electric, and manual. Hydraulic davits are powered by hydraulic fluid, ensuring a smooth operation and making them suitable for larger ships. Electric davits operate using electricity, allowing for quick and efficient lifting and launching of lifeboats. Manual davits, on the other hand, require manpower to operate. They are typically used in smaller vessels and require physical effort to lift and lower lifeboats. These different types cater to the varying needs of different ships and ensure the safe launch and retrieval of lifeboats.</p></p>
