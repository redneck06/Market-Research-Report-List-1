<p><p>The industrial floor cleaner market consists of three main types: floor scrubbers, floor sweepers, and vacuum cleaners. Floor scrubbers are machines equipped with brushes or pads that use water, detergent, or cleaning solution to scrub and remove dirt from floors. Floor sweepers, on the other hand, use brushes or rotating brooms to collect dust, debris, and small particles. Lastly, vacuum cleaners are machines that use suction to effectively gather and eliminate dirt, dust, and other contaminants from floors.</p></p>
