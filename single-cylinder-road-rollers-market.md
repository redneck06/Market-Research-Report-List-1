<p><p>Single-cylinder road rollers are heavy construction machines used to compact soil, gravel, asphalt, and concrete surfaces. They are classified into two main types: tamp drum and smooth drum. Tamp drum road rollers feature a padded drum which is suitable for cohesive soils and small aggregates, providing additional compaction force. Smooth drum rollers have a smooth steel drum that is effective for compacting granular materials and larger aggregates. Both types serve different purposes, allowing construction projects to achieve optimal compaction based on the specific properties of the materials being used.</p></p>
