<p><p>The disc dryers market can be categorized into different types based on technology and mounting. Under technology types, there are direct heated and indirect heated disc dryers. Direct heated dryers use hot air or gas directly to dry the material, while indirect heated dryers transfer heat to the material through a heated surface or medium. In terms of mounting types, disc dryers can be vertical or horizontal, indicating the orientation in which the drying process takes place. These variations cater to different industry needs and preferences for efficient drying solutions.</p></p>
