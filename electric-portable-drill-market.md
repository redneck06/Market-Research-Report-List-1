<p><p>The electric portable drill market consists of two types: cable type and wireless type. Cable type electric portable drills are powered by electricity through a cable that needs to be plugged into an outlet. They provide a constant power source and are suitable for prolonged use. On the other hand, wireless type electric portable drills are battery-operated and do not require a cable. These drills offer more mobility and flexibility as they can be used in various locations without the need for an outlet. However, their power may decrease over time as the battery drains.</p></p>
