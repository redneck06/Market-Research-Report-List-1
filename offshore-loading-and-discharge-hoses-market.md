<p><ul><li>Single Core</li><li>Dual Core</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The offshore loading and discharge hoses market offers two main types: single core and dual core hoses. Single core hoses consist of a single layer of steel wires or spiral wound cables, providing strength and flexibility for transferring fluids between offshore installations and vessels. Dual core hoses, on the other hand, have an additional inner hose layer that can accommodate multiple fluid types simultaneously. This makes them suitable for operations requiring the transfer of different fluids, such as oil and chemicals or oil and gas, within a single hose system.</p></p>
