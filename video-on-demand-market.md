<p><ul><li>Transactional Video on Demand (TVOD)</li><li>Subscription Video on Demand (SVOD)</li><li>Near-Video on Demand (NVOD)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Video on Demand (VOD) market types can be classified into various categories. Transactional Video on Demand (TVOD) enables users to pay for individual content items on a per-view basis. Subscription Video on Demand (SVOD) allows users to access unlimited content for a fixed subscription fee. Near-Video on Demand (NVOD) provides scheduled content access, repeating at regular intervals. Other VOD market types include Ad-Based Video on Demand (AVOD), where content is free but includes advertisements, and Free Video on Demand (FVOD), which offers select content for no charge. These market types cater to different user preferences and revenue models in the VOD space.</p></p>
