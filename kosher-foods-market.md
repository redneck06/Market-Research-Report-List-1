<p><p>Kosher foods can be categorized into three market types: Kosher Pareve, Kosher Meat, and Kosher Dairy. Kosher Pareve products are neither meat nor dairy, meaning they contain no animal-derived ingredients. Kosher Meat products comply with specific rules regarding the slaughtering process and the type of animals that can be consumed. Kosher Dairy products are made from the milk of kosher animals and are subject to regulations regarding the separation of meat and dairy. These market types ensure that kosher consumers can make informed choices that align with their dietary restrictions.</p></p>
