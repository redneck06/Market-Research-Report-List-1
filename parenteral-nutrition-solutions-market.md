<p><strong>The Parenteral Nutrition Solutions Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Large Volume Parenteral (LVP)</li><li>Small Volume Parenteral (SVP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The parenteral nutrition solutions market consists of two main types: Large Volume Parenteral (LVP) and Small Volume Parenteral (SVP). LVP refers to solutions that are administered in large quantities, typically exceeding 100 mL, through intravenous infusion. These solutions are commonly used for patients with significant nutrient deficiencies or who cannot consume food orally. On the other hand, SVP involves solutions that are administered in smaller quantities, usually less than 100 mL, and are often used for patients with less severe nutrient deficiencies or for short-term nutritional support.</p></p>
