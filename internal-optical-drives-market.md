<p><strong>The Internal Optical Drives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CD</li><li>DVD</li><li>BD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Internal optical drives are devices used in computer systems to read and write optical discs. They come in three types: CD, DVD, and BD. CD drives can read and write CDs, which have a storage capacity of up to 700 MB. DVD drives read and write both CDs and DVDs, which offer higher storage capacity of up to 4.7 GB or 8.5 GB (dual-layer). BD drives are capable of reading and writing CDs, DVDs, and Blu-ray discs, which provide the highest storage capacity of up to 50 GB (dual-layer) or 100 GB (triple-layer).</p></p>
