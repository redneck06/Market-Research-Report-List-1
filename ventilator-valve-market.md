<p><p>The Ventilator Valve market types include Solenoid Valve, Proportional Valve, and Others. Solenoid Valve is an electrically operated valve that controls the flow of gases. It allows for fast opening and closing, making it ideal for ventilator systems. Proportional Valve, on the other hand, offers variable control of gas flow by adjusting the valve opening in proportion to the input signal. Other types of valves in the market may include manual valves, check valves, or pressure relief valves, which serve specific functions in ventilator systems. These valves play a crucial role in regulating gas flow and maintaining the desired pressure during ventilation.</p></p>
