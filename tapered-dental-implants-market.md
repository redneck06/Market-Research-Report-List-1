<p><strong>The Tapered Dental Implants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Root-form Dental Implants</li><li>Plate-form Dental Implants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tapered dental implants are a type of dental implant used for replacing missing teeth. These implants have a tapered shape, resembling the root of a natural tooth, and are designed to provide stability and support to the artificial tooth. Root-form dental implants are the most common type of tapered implant and are directly implanted into the jawbone. Plate-form dental implants, on the other hand, are used in cases where the jawbone is too narrow for a root-form implant, and a plate-like structure is placed on top of the jawbone to support the artificial tooth.</p></p>
