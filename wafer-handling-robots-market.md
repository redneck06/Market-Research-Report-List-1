<p><strong>The Wafer Handling Robots Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Atmosheric Transfer Robot</li><li>Vacuum Transfer Robot</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Wafer Handling Robots Market is categorized into two types: Atmospheric Transfer Robot and Vacuum Transfer Robot. Atmospheric Transfer Robots are designed to handle wafers in a standard atmospheric environment. They use gripper blades to securely hold and transport wafers. On the other hand, Vacuum Transfer Robots are equipped with vacuum arm technology to handle wafers in a controlled vacuum environment. They create a vacuum between the wafer and the robot's arm, ensuring a reliable and contamination-free transfer. Both types of robots play a crucial role in the efficient and precise handling of wafers in various industries.</p></p>
