<p><p>The RF fixed attenuators market includes three main types: low power attenuators, medium power attenuators, and high power attenuators. High power attenuators are specifically designed to handle significantly higher power levels, often exceeding 10 watts. These three types cater to different power requirements and help in reducing signal power levels in RF systems with varying power needs.</p></p>
