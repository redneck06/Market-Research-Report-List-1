<p><p>Water-Cooled Scroll Chillers and Air-Cooled Scroll Chillers are two types of Scroll Chiller markets. Water-Cooled Scroll Chillers use water as a cooling medium to transfer heat from the chiller to the environment, making them suitable for applications where water is readily available. On the other hand, Air-Cooled Scroll Chillers use air as a cooling medium and do not require a constant water supply, making them more flexible in terms of installation. Both types of chillers are used to cool various industrial processes and equipment, providing efficient and reliable cooling solutions.</p></p>
