<p><p>The digital cinema lens market comprises two types: prime lenses and zoom lenses. Prime lenses have a fixed focal length, offering a single magnification level, resulting in enhanced image quality and low light performance. They are commonly used in static shooting scenarios and deliver sharper and clearer images. Zoom lenses, on the other hand, provide variable focal lengths, enabling the adjustment of magnification levels while filming. They offer versatility and convenience, allowing cinematographers to capture a wider range of shots without the need to change lenses frequently.</p></p>
