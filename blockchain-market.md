<p><ul><li>Public Blockchain</li><li>Private Blockchain</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Public Blockchain is a decentralized network where anyone can participate, view, and validate transactions. It is open to the public, ensuring transparency and trustworthiness. It is often used in cryptocurrencies and open-source projects.</p><p>On the other hand, Private Blockchain is a restricted network controlled by a particular organization. Only authorized participants can join and access transaction details. It offers more privacy and control but sacrifices some decentralization and transparency. Private blockchains are commonly utilized in industries such as finance, healthcare, and supply chain management.</p></p>
