<p><p>Foam chambers are used in fire protection systems to deliver foam agents to extinguish fires. There are two common types: square foam chamber and round foam chamber. The square foam chamber is designed to deliver foam in a rectangular pattern, covering a larger area. On the other hand, the round foam chamber delivers foam in a circular pattern, suitable for protecting smaller areas. Both types work in conjunction with foam concentrates and fire sprinkler systems to suppress fires by forming a thick foam blanket over the fuel surface.</p></p>
