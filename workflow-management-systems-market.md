<p><p>Workflow Management Systems (WMS) are software applications that facilitate the automation and coordination of tasks within organizations. The WMS market is divided into two main types: Cloud-based and On-Premises models.</p><p>Cloud-based WMS is a type of software that is hosted and operated by third-party service providers. It allows organizations to access and use the system over the internet, eliminating the need for on-site infrastructure maintenance. On the other hand, the On-Premises model involves having the WMS software installed and operated on the organization's internal servers.</p><p>Both models offer benefits such as improved efficiency, streamlined processes, and increased productivity. The choice between Cloud-based and On-Premises WMS depends on factors like budget, data security requirements, scalability, and organizational preferences.</p></p>
