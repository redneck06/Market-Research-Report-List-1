<p><p>Hearing screening apparatus market can be categorized into three main types: Otoacoustic Emissions (OAE), Auditory Brainstem Response (ABR), and Other types. OAE tests measure the sounds produced by the inner ear in response to external stimuli, while ABR tests measure the electrical activity in the brain in response to sound. Other types of screening apparatus may include combination devices or newer technologies that offer different methods of detecting hearing loss. Each type of apparatus has its own strengths and limitations in terms of accuracy and ease of use.</p></p>
