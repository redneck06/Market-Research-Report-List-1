<p><strong>The Agricultural Wastewater Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-submersible</li><li>Submersible</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Non-submersible agricultural wastewater pumps are installed outside of the water source and are used for transferring wastewater from one location to another. They are more commonly used for surface water applications due to their ability to handle larger volumes of water. </p><p>On the other hand, submersible agricultural wastewater pumps are installed directly into the water source and are submerged during operation. These pumps are more suitable for deep water applications and are more energy-efficient compared to non-submersible pumps.</p></p>
