<p><p>Broadband CPE (Customer Premises Equipment) encompasses various market types, including Cable CPE, DSL CPE, Fiber-optic CPE, and Others. Cable CPE refers to devices that connect to cable networks to provide internet access, such as cable modems. DSL CPE enables broadband access through digital subscriber lines, utilizing DSL modems. Fiber-optic CPE involves devices like optical network terminals connecting homes and businesses to fiber-optic networks. Other CPE types may comprise satellite modems, wireless routers, or Ethernet switches. Each of these market types represents specific equipment utilized to establish broadband connectivity based on different technologies.</p></p>
