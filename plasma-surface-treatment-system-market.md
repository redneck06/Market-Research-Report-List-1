<p><p>The Plasma Surface Treatment System market consists of two main types: Atmospheric Pressure Type and Low Pressure/Vacuum Plasma Type. The Atmospheric Pressure Type operates at normal atmospheric pressure, which is suitable for continuous production lines and high-speed processes. On the other hand, the Low Pressure/Vacuum Plasma Type operates in a low-pressure or vacuum environment, allowing for a more precise control of the plasma.
