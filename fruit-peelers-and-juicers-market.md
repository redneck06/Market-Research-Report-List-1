<p><p>Fruit peelers and juicers come in three market types: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manual peelers and juicers require human effort and control throughout the peeling and juicing process. Semi-automatic machines partially automate the process, reducing human effort but still requiring some manual intervention. Fully automatic machines are highly mechanized, performing all peeling and juicing functions without human assistance.
