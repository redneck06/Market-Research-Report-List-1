<p><p>Termite bait system products consist of two main market types: bait devices and liquid eradication methods. Bait devices are designed to attract termites and contain a mixture of cellulose and toxic chemicals. These baits are strategically placed around the perimeter of a property to lure termites away from the building. On the other hand, liquid eradication methods involve the application of liquid termiticides to affected areas. These chemicals create a barrier that termites cannot penetrate, effectively eliminating the infestation. Both methods are used for termite control and prevention.</p></p>
