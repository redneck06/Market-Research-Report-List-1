<p><p>The Anti-thyroid Peroxidase Antibody Test Kit market encompasses two types of testing methods: Chemiluminescence Immunoassay and Time-resolved Immunoassay. Chemiluminescence Immunoassay detects the presence of anti-thyroid peroxidase antibodies by measuring the light produced during a chemical reaction. Time-resolved Immunoassay works by using a fluorescent label that emits light for a specific period, allowing for the detection of anti-thyroid peroxidase antibodies. These testing methods play a crucial role in identifying autoimmune thyroid diseases and aid in providing accurate diagnoses.</p></p>
