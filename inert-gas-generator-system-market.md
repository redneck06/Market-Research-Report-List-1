<p><p>The inert gas generator system (IGGS) market encompasses three main types: aviation IGGS, marine IGGS, and industrial IGGS. Aviation IGGS refers to systems used in aircraft to prevent fuel tank explosions by displacing oxygen with inert gas. Marine IGGS are utilized on ships to reduce the risk of fire or explosion by creating an inert atmosphere in cargo spaces or tanks. Industrial IGGS are employed in various industries, such as oil and gas, for purging or blanketing applications to prevent oxidation or explosion hazards. Each type serves the purpose of enhancing safety by minimizing the presence of oxygen.</p></p>
