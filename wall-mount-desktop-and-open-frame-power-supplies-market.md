<p><p>Wall mount, desktop, and open frame power supplies are electronic devices used to convert electrical energy from a power source into usable power for electronic devices and appliances. Each type has its own unique features and applications.</p><p>Wall mount power supplies are designed to be mounted on walls or other surfaces, providing a convenient and space-saving solution for powering devices such as routers, modems, and small appliances. They are widely used in residential and commercial settings.</p><p>Desktop power supplies are larger than wall mount versions and are intended to sit on a desk or table. They are commonly used to power desktop computers, gaming consoles, and other electronic devices that require higher power levels.</p><p>Open frame power supplies are typically used in industrial applications and are designed without an enclosure or housing. They are modular in nature, allowing for easy integration into different system setups.</p><p>The market for wall mount, desktop, and open frame power supplies is expected to grow at a remarkable CAGR of 11.2% during the forecast period.
