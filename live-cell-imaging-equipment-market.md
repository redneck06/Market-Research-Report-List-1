<p><ul><li>Confocal Laser Scanning Microscopes</li><li>Spinning-Disk Confocal Microscopes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Live cell imaging equipment refers to advanced microscopy devices that enable the visualization of dynamic processes occurring within living cells. There are two main types of equipment in this market: confocal laser scanning microscopes and spinning-disk confocal microscopes. Confocal laser scanning microscopes use lasers to scan samples point-by-point, providing high-resolution 3D images with precise optical sectioning. On the other hand, spinning-disk confocal microscopes use spinning disks with multiple pinholes to provide rapid imaging of thicker samples, enabling real-time observation of cellular processes. Both types of equipment are crucial in modern cell biology and biomedical research.</p></p>
