<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Vibration welding machines are used for joining plastic components. There are two types of vibration welding machines commonly used in the market: linear and orbital. </p><p>Linear vibration welding machines oscillate the parts to be joined in a linear motion, rubbing them together to create enough heat for fusion. This type is preferred for small and medium-sized parts.</p><p>In contrast, orbital vibration welding machines move the parts in a circular motion, generating friction and heat for fusion. This type is suitable for larger parts or components that require a more precise welding process.</p></p>
