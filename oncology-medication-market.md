<p><p>The oncology medication market consists of various types of medications used to treat cancer. Chemotherapy medication involves the use of drugs that kill or stop the growth of cancer cells throughout the body. Targeted therapy medication focuses on specific molecules within cancer cells to inhibit their growth and spread. Immunotherapy medication stimulates the body's immune system to fight cancer cells. Hormonal therapy medication is used to block or lower the levels of hormones that aid in cancer's growth. Other medications include angiogenesis inhibitors, radiotherapy drugs, and supportive care medications.</p></p>
