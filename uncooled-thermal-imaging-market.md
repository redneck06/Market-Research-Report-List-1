<p><strong>The Uncooled Thermal Imaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short Wave IR</li><li>Mid Wave IR</li><li>Long Wave IR</li><li>Far Wave IR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The uncooled thermal imaging market comprises four main types: Short Wave IR, Mid Wave IR, Long Wave IR, and Far Wave IR. Short Wave IR captures wavelengths between 0.9 and 1.7 micrometers, suitable for high-resolution imaging and atmospheric penetration. Mid Wave IR captures wavelengths between 3 and 5 micrometers, offering good image quality and detection of various gases. Long Wave IR captures wavelengths between 8 and 14 micrometers, rendering high-resolution and accurate temperature measurements. Far Wave IR captures wavelengths above 15 micrometers, benefiting from lower atmospheric absorption for long-range applications.</p></p>
