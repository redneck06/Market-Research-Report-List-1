<p><ul><li>Active Blue Roof System</li><li>Passive Blue Roof System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Blue Roof System market encompasses two types: Active Blue Roof System and Passive Blue Roof System. Active Blue Roof Systems are designed to actively manage and control stormwater by using technologies such as pumps and sensors. These systems provide real-time monitoring and can actively regulate the flow of water. On the other hand, Passive Blue Roof Systems are more traditional and rely on the natural features of a rooftop to control stormwater. These systems use rooftop gardens and other green infrastructure to absorb and store water, reducing the impact of heavy rainfall.</p></p>
