<p><strong>The Dental Air Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal Spray</li><li>External Spray</li><li>Without Spray</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Dental Air Motor market consists of three types: Internal Spray, External Spray, and Without Spray. In the Internal Spray market, the air motor is embedded with a spray channel that delivers water or coolant directly to the instrument. This helps to maintain proper cooling and irrigation during dental procedures. In the External Spray market, the air motor is equipped with an external nozzle that provides water or coolant to the instrument. This type ensures effective cooling and irrigation. Lastly, the Without Spray market includes air motors that don't have a built-in spray system, requiring separate cooling methods during dental procedures.</p></p>
