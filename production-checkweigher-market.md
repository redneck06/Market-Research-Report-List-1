<p><ul><li>In-Motion Checkweighers</li><li>Intermittent Checkweighers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Production checkweighers are devices used in manufacturing processes to ensure the weight of packaged products is within specified limits. In-motion checkweighers weigh products as they move on a conveyor belt, offering continuous and real-time monitoring. Intermittent checkweighers operate by stopping the conveyor belt to weigh individual products, making them suitable for slower production lines. Both types provide accurate weight measurements and can also detect under or overweight items, helping manufacturers maintain quality control and optimize production efficiency.</p></p>
