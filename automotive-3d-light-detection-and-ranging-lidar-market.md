<p><p>Automotive 3D LiDAR technology is used for creating high-resolution 3D maps of the environment around a vehicle to enable autonomous driving. Two main types are Solid State LiDAR and Mechanical/Scanning LiDAR. Solid State LiDAR uses fixed sensors to scan the surroundings, offering a more compact and reliable solution. Mechanical/Scanning LiDAR, on the other hand, uses moving parts to direct laser pulses, allowing for a wider field of view but with potentially higher maintenance needs. Both types are crucial for the development of self-driving cars.</p></p>
