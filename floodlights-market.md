<p><ul><li>Metal-halide Lamp Type</li><li>LED Lamp Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The floodlights market comprises two major types: metal-halide lamp type and LED lamp type. Metal-halide lamp floodlights use a mixture of gases and metals to produce a bright white light with high luminous efficacy. They are commonly used in outdoor lighting applications. On the other hand, LED lamp floodlights use light-emitting diodes to produce light. LED floodlights are energy-efficient, have a long lifespan, and offer excellent color rendering. This type of floodlight is increasingly popular due to its eco-friendly characteristics and versatility in various settings.</p></p>
