<p><p>The fishing cooler box market can be divided into two main types: hard cooler and soft cooler market. Hard coolers are typically made of sturdy materials like plastic or metal and provide excellent insulation to keep the contents cold for an extended period. They are durable and can withstand harsh conditions, making them ideal for longer fishing trips. On the other hand, soft coolers are made with flexible fabrics and insulation, offering portability and versatility.
