<p><p>Automated Urine Analyzers are medical devices used to analyze urine samples quickly and accurately. There are two main types of Urine Analyzers: Chemistry Method and Microscopy Method. The Chemistry Method utilizes automated systems to measure various chemical components in urine, such as glucose, protein, and pH levels. This method helps detect diseases like diabetes and kidney disorders. On the other hand, the Microscopy Method involves automated microscopy systems that examine urine samples to identify the presence of abnormal cells, bacteria, or sediment, aiding in diagnosing infections or other urinary tract issues.</p></p>
