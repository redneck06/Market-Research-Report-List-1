<p><p>Venous Thromboembolism Therapeutics Market is segmented into different types based on the drugs that are used for treatment. Factor Xa inhibitors, such as rivaroxaban and apixaban, work by blocking the activity of Factor Xa, an enzyme involved in blood clot formation. Direct Thrombin Inhibitors, like dabigatran, directly inhibit the action of thrombin, a protein essential in clot formation. Heparin, a commonly used anticoagulant, prevents clotting by inhibiting clotting factors. Vitamin K antagonists, such as warfarin, interfere with the production of clotting factors. Other drugs used for VTE treatment include tissue plasminogen activators and antiplatelet drugs.</p></p>
