<p><p>The waste collection vehicle market consists of three main types: front loaders, rear loaders, and side loaders. Front loaders are equipped with an automated arm that lifts and empties waste containers into the vehicle from the front. Rear loaders have a large opening at the back, enabling workers to manually load waste into the vehicle. Side loaders feature an arm or conveyor system on the vehicle's side, which lifts and empties bins mechanically. Each type has its own advantages and is used based on the specific needs and preferences of waste collection operations.</p></p>
