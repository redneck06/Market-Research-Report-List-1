<p><p>Tenant screening services refer to the process of evaluating potential tenants to assess their suitability for renting a property. Firstly, the increasing number of rental properties and the surging demand for rental accommodations are driving the need for effective tenant screening processes. Landlords desire to protect their properties and ensure a safe and secure living environment for existing tenants.</p><p>Technological advancements have also played a crucial role in the growth of the tenant screening services market.
