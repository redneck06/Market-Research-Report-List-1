<p><p>The Ambulatory Infusion Therapy Market consists of two main types: Infusion Pumps and Intravenous Sets. Infusion pumps are devices that deliver fluids, such as medications and nutrients, directly into a patient's bloodstream. Intravenous sets are tubing systems that connect the infusion pump to the patient and allow the fluid to flow into their veins. Additionally, the market includes other devices like catheters and needles. The Infusion Therapy Service market encompasses the support and services provided during the infusion process, such as nurse monitoring, education, and home healthcare services.</p></p>
