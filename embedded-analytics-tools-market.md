<p><p>Embedded analytics tools refer to software solutions that integrate analytics capabilities within other applications or systems. The market is classified into two main types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based tools enable users to access analytics capabilities through the internet, eliminating the need for physical servers or infrastructure. They provide flexibility, scalability, and easy collaboration. On premise tools, on the other hand, are installed and operated within the organization's infrastructure. They offer greater control and customization but require maintenance and hardware investments. Both markets provide organizations with data insights and decision-making capabilities to enhance business performance.</p></p>
